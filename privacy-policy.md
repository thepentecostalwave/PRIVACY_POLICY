## 2. What the App does with data

### 2.1 Guest / offline use (default)

When you continue as **Guest**:

- Cashbooks, entries, categories, reminders, recurring rules, and settings are stored in a **local database on your phone**.
- This data **does not leave your device** through the App for cloud backup (Guest mode has no cloud storage).
- We do **not** require an account for Guest use.

You control this data. Uninstalling the App, clearing app storage, or resetting the device may permanently delete local data unless you have exported a backup.

### 2.2 Sign-in / account (when enabled)

If you choose to **sign in** or **register** when cloud features are available:

- You may provide an **email address** and **password** (or other credentials required by the sign-in service).
- Account features may include **cloud storage backup and restore** across devices, as described in the App.
- Guest data already on the phone may be kept when you register, as stated in the App.

Cloud services (if enabled later) will process account and backup data according to this policy and any third-party provider terms we disclose in an update.

### 2.3 Export and share

If you use **Export**, **Share**, **Import**, or **Print / PDF**:

- You choose where files go (for example email, messaging apps, Files, or a printer).
- Those destinations are controlled by **you** and by the apps or services you select — not by us beyond creating the file on your request.

### 2.4 App lock

If you enable **app lock** (biometric / PIN), that protection uses your device’s system security. We do not receive your PIN or biometric templates.

## 3. Types of information

| Type | Examples | Where it lives |
|------|----------|----------------|
| Financial entries you enter | Amounts, dates, descriptions, notes, categories, payment modes | On your device (local database) |
| Cashbook setup | Book names, opening balances, default book | On your device |
| Preferences | Theme, font size, language, lock setting | On your device |
| Account credentials (optional) | Email / password when sign-in is used | Device + cloud provider (when enabled) |
| Backup files you export | XML / CSV / printed reports | Wherever you save or share them |

We do **not** intentionally collect sensitive personal categories such as government ID numbers, precise continuous location, or contacts unless you type them yourself into notes or descriptions.

---

## 4. How we use information

We use information only to:

- Provide cashbook features (balances, reports, categories, reminders, settings)
- Save your preferences on the device
- Confirm important actions (for example MMSS confirmation codes generated on device)
- Enable optional account / cloud backup when you choose those features
- Improve reliability and fix bugs (if you contact support and share logs or screenshots yourself)

We do **not** sell your personal or financial data.

---

## 5. Sharing with others

We do **not** share your cashbook entries with advertisers or data brokers.

Data may leave your device only when:

- **You** export, share, print, or send a file
- **You** use sign-in / cloud backup (when available), in which case data is processed by the cloud provider needed to run that feature
- Required by law (for example a valid legal request)

Android system services (Play Store updates, device backup if you enable Google backup, etc.) are governed by Google’s and your device maker’s policies.

---

## 6. Data retention

- **On device:** Kept until you delete entries, reset sample data, clear app storage, or uninstall the App.
- **Exports you create:** Retained wherever you store them; we do not control those copies.
- **Cloud backups (when enabled):** Retained while your account and backup service keep them; you may request deletion via the in-app account options or by contacting us.

## 7. Children

The App is a general finance tool and is **not directed at children under 13** (or the minimum age required in your country). Do not use the App to store a child’s personal data if you are not permitted to do so.

## 8. Security

We use normal Android app storage practices for local data. No method of storage is 100% secure. You can reduce risk by:

- Enabling app lock
- Keeping your device updated
- Exporting backups you control
- Using a strong password if you sign in
